Won-Buddhism

Meditation Temple, Chapel Hill, NC

Welcome to the website
of the Won-Buddhism Meditation Temple of North Carolina.
We come together for meditation and dharma practice every Saturday & Sunday at 10am and during the week. For full program descriptions & schedules CLICK HERE.
Our Temple & Meditation Center are open to everyone; families, students, and those who are "just curious" are welcomed. No experience is necessary. There are no fees for our programs, however, the temple is fully self-supporting and any level of donations are much appreciated.
The Temple is always open,
including holidays and even during inclement weather.
