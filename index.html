Won-Buddhism

Meditation Temple, Chapel Hill, NC

Welcome to the website
of the Won-Buddhism Meditation Temple of North Carolina.
We come together for meditation and dharma practice every Saturday & Sunday at 10am and at various times during the week.
For full program descriptions & schedules CLICK HERE.

Our Temple & Meditation Center are open to everyone; families, students, and those who are "just curious" are welcomed. No experience is necessary. There are no fees for our programs, however, the temple is fully self-supporting and any level of donations are much appreciated.
Special Event - Visiting Won Buddishm Scholar - Professor Bongkil Chung
Nov 1-4. Prof. Chung will be giving several talks throughout the weekend.

Click Here to see details and full schedule.

  • A very special visitor is coming to our Won Buddhist Temple in November.
    Professor Bongkil Chung, who wrote: The Scriptures of Won Buddhism: A
    Translation of Wonbulgyo kyojon with Introduction and The Dharma Words of
    Master Chongsan is coming to teach us.

    This is a wonderful opportunity to learn from a Won Buddhist Scholar who has
    taught in the United States for over 30 years.

    Professor Chung will speak:

    Thursday Evening, Nov. 1, from 7-8:30: Enlightenment to the truth of Il Won
    Sang

    Friday Evening, Nov. 2, from 7-8:30: How to practice the truth of Il Won
    Sang.

    Saturday and Sunday during our regular Dharma Services from 10AM to 12 Noon:
    My special experiences with Master ChongSan.

    Saturday Afternoon, Nov. 3, from 2PM to 4PM: Taoism, Confucianism, Buddhism
    and Won Buddhism.

    • Everyone is welcome!

The Temple is always open,
