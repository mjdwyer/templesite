Some Thoughts & Questions About Your Marriage

  • Are your marital problems the excuses you use as to why your marriage is falling apart, or are they the opportunity to take your marriage to the next level?

    You can either work hard at being happy and strong or you can work hard at being weak and miserable – the amount of work is the same.

    Lead by example, request what you want and employ consequences, both positive and negative, as a motivator to induce change.

    Your anger is a natural resource - use it wisely.

    I am your ally not your adversary. If I appear to be your adversary it is because I don’t fully understand myself; meaning, the obstacles I have yet to resolve.

    When your life experiences intertwine and compliment each other you have the raw material for “marital bliss”; meaning, you gain a deep appreciation for your partner. When your life experiences collide, you have confusion, shock, loss of direction and a sense of failure.
