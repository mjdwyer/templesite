Welcome

Go to Slide Show

Welcome to the website of the Chapel Hill Won-Buddhism Meditation Temple. We come together for meditation/dharma practice every Saturday and Sunday at 10:00 am (directions). The Temple is open to everyone. Families, students, and "just curious" are welcome. No experience necessary.

The Temple is always open, including holidays and even during inclement weather.

On-going Meditation

Of course, any time is a good time to meditate! Please contact us if you would like to have individual meditation or with your family. But you are very welcome to join the group practice at one of these times:

  • Sunday: 10:00 am
  • Saturday: 10:00 am
  • Tuesday Evening: 7:30-8:30 pm
  • Every Morning : 6:30 am
