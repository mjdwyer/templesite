We are a Federaly Approved Charity

The Temple has recently been added to the list of approved charities in the Combined Federal Campaign (CFC). As all Federal employees know, the CFC is an opportunity to give to your preferred charities through automatic payroll deduction. If you are a Federal employee, we hope you will consider Won-Buddhism of NC in your charitable plan. Encouraging your Federal employee friends to do so would also be appreciated and would foster the programs conducted by the Temple.
