Upcoming Events & Ongoing Meditation

On going meditation

Of course, any time is a good time to meditate! Please contact us if you would like to have individual meditation or with your family. But you are very welcome to join the group practice at one of these times:

  • Sunday: 10:00 am
  • Saturday: 10:00 am
  • Tuesday Evening: 7:30-8:30 pm
  • Every Morning (Monday - Saturday): 6:30 am

Monthly Saturday Afernoon Retreat

Every last Saturday of the month from 2pm - 5pm. Features green tea, walking, moving chanting and quiet sitting meditation. RSVP or sign up would be appreciated. Suggested donation $10. Children/Family Meditation (ages 5–12)

First Sunday of every month:

3 PM - A brief game/activity for children followed by the meditation session.

Green Tea Meditation with Kyomunim

We also invite you to come for green tea meditation and conversation. Please call us to let us know when is a good time for you or your family (children are welcome) to visit.
