Welcome to the website of the Chapel Hill Won-Buddhism Meditation Temple. We come together for meditation practice every Saturday at 10:00 am and Sunday at 10:00 am (directions). The Temple is open to everyone. Families, students, and "just curious" are welcome. No experience necessary.

The Temple is always open, including holidays and even during inclement weather.
