Special Event

6. 1 Memorial Day (7 PM, Friday, June 1)

Sotaesan entered nirvana on June 1 in 1943, after his dedicated dharma work. On this night, we recognize Sotaesan and all of the buddhas, sages, saints, spiritual leaders who passed after their life time dedication of enlightening us and alleviating sufferings in the world. We will have meditation and readings from different teachers of the past.

Children are welcome. Light refreshments will be served.


Continuing our memorial service...

Drum Circle Night

Join us June 2 from 7:30 to 8:30 p.m. Saturday night for a community drum circle at the Won Buddhism Temple. All levels of drummers, chanters and listeners are welcome to join us in a community of rhythm. We will dedicate this drum circle to the celebration of our community and the memory of John Hartely who built our beautiful dharma hall. This hall was John’s last work before he died a year ago this weekend. We will start with gentle drumming, chanting, meditation in the dharma hall followed by outdoor drum circle.

No experience necessary. Bring drums, tambourines, triangles or other percussion instruments. If you have extras, bring them to share. If you have any questions, contact Michele at micheledrostin@gmail.com or the Temple (919-933-6946).
