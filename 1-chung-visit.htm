Special Event

Visiting Won Buddishm Scholar - Professor Bongkil Chung

Nov 1-4. Prof. Chung will be giving several talks throughout the weekend.

A very special visitor is coming to our Won Buddhist Temple in November.
Professor Bongkil Chung, who wrote: The Scriptures of Won Buddhism: A Translation of Wonbulgyo kyojon with Introduction and The Dharma Words of Master Chongsan is coming to teach us.

This is a wonderful opportunity to learn from a Won Buddhist Scholar who has taught in the United States for over 30 years.

Professor Chung will speak:

Thursday Evening, Nov. 1, from 7-8:30: Enlightenment to the truth of Il Won Sang

Friday Evening, Nov. 2, from 7-8:30: How to practice the truth of Il Won Sang.

Saturday and Sunday during our regular Dharma Services from 10AM to 12 Noon:
My special experiences with Master ChongSan.

Saturday Afternoon, Nov. 3, from 2PM to 4PM: Taoism, Confucianism, Buddhism and Won Buddhism.

Everyone is welcome!
