Won-Buddhism

Meditation Temple, Chapel Hill, NC


Welcome to the website of the Chapel Hill Won-Buddhism Meditation Temple. We come together for meditation and dharma practice every Saturday and Sunday at 10:00 am and during the week (see our programs page for full program schedules). The Temple is open to everyone. Families, students, and "just curious" are welcome. No experience is necessary and all programs are free.

The Temple is always open, including holidays and even during inclement weather.
