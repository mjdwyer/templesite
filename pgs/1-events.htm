Ongoing Meditation

Of course, any time is a good time to meditate! You are welcome to schedule a time if you would like to have individual meditation or with your family. You are also very welcome to join us in group meditation at these times:

Individuals are welcome to come during daylights hours for their own quiet time or meditation (when there are no temple programs scheduled).

Monthly Meditations & Activities

Although there are no fees for these programs, the Temple is self-supporting and any level of donations are always welcomed and appreciated!

  • 1st Sunday, 3pm: Children/Family Meditation (ages 5–12)

    A brief game/activity for children followed by the meditation session.

  • 2nd Sunday, 5pm: Teen Meditation

    A meditation time geared toward teenagers.

  • 3rd Thursday, 7pm: Informal Dharma Discussion

    Various topics are choosen each month. An announcement of the topic is sent to those who subscribe to the newsletter.

  • 2nd Friday, 6-8pm: Raleigh Meditation-Led by Rev. WonGong & Rev. Lee

    Given at Moving Mantra Yoga Studio - 200 Sawmill Rd. Suite 101, Raleigh - www.movingmantrayoga.com - (919) 449-0530
