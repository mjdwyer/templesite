Ongoing Meditation, Programs & Events

Ongoing Meditation

Of course, any time is a good time to meditate! Please contact us if you would like to have individual meditation or with your family. You are also very welcome to join the group practice at these times:

  • Sunday: 10am-12pm
  • Saturday: 10am-12pm
  • Tuesday Evening: 7:30-8:30 pm
  • Every Morning (Monday - Saturday): 6:30-7:30 am
Monday yoga night guided by Fran Richmond (5:45 - 7PM)
Special Monthly Meditations & Programs

Children/Family Meditation (ages 5–12) 1st Sunday of every month: 3 PM:

A brief game/activity for children followed by the meditation session.

Teen Meditation: 2nd Sunday of every month, 5pm:

A meditation time geared toward teenagers.

Last Saturday Afternoon Retreat

Every last Saturday of the month from 2pm - 5pm. Features green tea, walking, moving chanting and quiet sitting meditation. A RSVP or a sign up would be appreciated. Suggested donation $10.

Raleigh Meditation - Second Friday of Every Month 6-8 PM

at Moving Mantra Yoga Studio - 200 Sawmill Rd. Suite 101, Raleigh - www.movingmantrayoga.com - (919) 449.0530 - Led by Rev. WonGong.

Thursday Evening Programs:

3rd Thursday-7pm, Informal Dharma Discussion

Green Tea Meditation with Kyomunim

We also invite you to come for green tea meditation and conversation. Please call us to let us know when is a good time for you or your family (children are welcome) to visit.
