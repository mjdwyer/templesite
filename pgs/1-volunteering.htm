Volunteer Opportunities

We are looking for volunteers who would like to join us in a working meditation by supporting our temple and to build good Karma and share in the joy of service to others.

No amount of time is too small!!!

Here are a few areas in which we could use your service.

  • Zen gardening
  • Ground care
  • Cleaning
  • Bringing snacks for after services
  • Children program volunteer

Please contact us via email at wontempleNC@gmail.com and tell us a little about your interests, and how much time you might be interested in volunteering, and we will get back to you with more details.
