Temple Wish List

 

You are welcome to support the Temple by providing any of the following items on the Temple Wish List.
  • Tea lights candles
  • Water softener salt
  • Electrical hedge trimmer
  • Snacks for after meditation and special events
This Temple is self supporting through your donations alone. Your contributions allow the Temple to continue its programs and outreach. Any level of contribution is appreciated.
Please make checks payable to - Won-Buddhism of NC. Donors are sent a tax receipt letter.
