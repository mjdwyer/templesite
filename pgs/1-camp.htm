Summer Dharma Camps
Our Children's Dharma Camp will be
June 11- June 15 8:30am- 4:30pm. Ages 6-12
Teen’s Dharma Camp: June 18 – 21 (Mon-Thur, 8:30 AM – 4:30 PM) Ages 13-17

 

Programs:

  • Tai Chi, Yoga, sitting, walking and lying down meditation, chanting and singing,
  • green tea, mindfulness, dharma play, games,
  • TaeKwonDo, cooking, journal writing, art/craft projects, and more.

If you are interested in sending your child or grandchild to this camp, please fill out the application and mail it with a check. If you have a question, feel free to contact us.
