About the Temple

We are the Won-Buddhism Meditation Temple of Chapel Hill. Upcoming events and general information about weekly activities can be found HERE. Our Temple & Meditation Center are open to everyone; families, students, and those who are "just curious" are welcomed. No experience is necessary. There are no fees for our programs, however, the temple is fully self-supporting and any level of donations are much appreciated. Please join us!

Frequently Asked Questions

What Is Won-Buddhism?

Won-Buddhism was founded in 1916 in Korea, when Grand Master Sotaesan achieved his great enlightenment. Conceived as a contemporary form of Buddhism, Won-Buddhism incorporates essential principles of Confucianism, Buddhism, and Taoism. In our Temple, we practice different forms of meditation such as moving, chanting, sitting, and reflection on daily life, learning to cultivate "True Spirit, Wisdom, and Mindful Choice in Action".
