Special Event
Won Meditation Temple Music Concert
Friday, March 23, 7:30 - 8:45pm

To benefit the Children's Dharma Camp Scholarship and Dharma Retreat Fund.

Suggested donation: $10/person ($5 for children)

Featured Performances

  • A Capella of dharma songs and Korean opera performed by the Singing KyoMuNyms (Wongong and her sister, Julia)
  • “Moon Halo” song composed by Wongong
  • Singing and guitar duo (Grace Camblos and Bill Whitmore)
  • Classical piano music by Peter Smith (8th grade)
  • Singing by a singing guitarist, Donna Moldovan
  • Group chanting with audience participation guided Drew Pilant

Proceeds will provide scholarship funds in support of children who wish to attend the Won Dharma Day Camp in June, in addition to supporting adults in need of financial assistance for overnight dharma retreats. Please email or call to reserve your seat.
